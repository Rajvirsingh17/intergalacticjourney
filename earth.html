Earth: The Blue Jewel of the Galaxy

Earth is home to a diverse, technologically advanced civilization that spans the globe. Over centuries, it has transitioned from a fractured collection of nations into a united, peaceful federation. Earth is the cradle of cutting-edge research, sustainable technologies, and a beacon of hope in the galaxy.

The United Earth Federation (UEF) was formed in the early 22nd century, consolidating the planet's resources to focus on space exploration, renewable energy, and global peacekeeping. Earth has now become the central hub for intergalactic diplomacy, trade, and collaboration.
