Welcome to the Interplanetary Journey

Explore the planets in our solar system and beyond. Each of these worlds has its unique history and fascinating stories that define their existence in the galaxy.

The galaxy was once a united front, with planets coexisting under the banner of a single, albeit fragile, peace treaty. However, as technological advancements have shifted the balance of power, old alliances have fractured, and new tensions have emerged. The discovery of intergalactic portals has expanded the reach of these worlds, leading to a flourishing of trade and cultural exchange. But with that prosperity comes distrust and political intrigue. The growing unrest across all the planets—ranging from the Light Planet’s political uprisings to the Red Planet’s aggressive military stance and the Dark Planet’s internal magical conflicts—threatens to plunge the universe into war. Earth, situated in the middle of these cosmic rivalries, finds itself at the crossroads of destiny, struggling to maintain peace, while mysterious forces—both human and non-human—seek to tip the scales of power in their favor.
